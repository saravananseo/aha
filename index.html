📐 Month Calculation Rule
Count complete months, then check remaining days.
If remaining days ≥ 5 → add 1 extra month.
✓ 13 Jan → 7 Mar = 1 month + 22 days → 2 months
✓ 1 Jan → 6 Mar = 2 months + 5 days → 3 months
✗ 1 Jan → 4 Mar = 2 months + 3 days → 2 months
💡 Simple Interest Formula
Interest = Principal × (Rate ÷ 100) × Months
Total Due = Principal + Interest
